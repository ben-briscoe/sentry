# Surveilance & ENforcement Response SYstem (SENTRY)

INSY 6130 group project for Team 15

## Vision Statement

SENTRY will be an unmanned aerial system (UAS) that provides persistent, automated surveillance of crowd-control perimeters, detecting and reporting boundary crossings in real time to give dispath and ground forces a clear, shared operational picture. When limited incursions occur, it will be able to support de-escalation and response with non-lethal deterrence options (e.g. spotlighting, audible warnings, optical dazzling, andd/or high power microwaves).

## CONOPS Statement

SENTRY will operatee as an overwatch UAS assigned to a defined crowd-control perimeter. It will continuously monitor barrier lines and adjacent approaches using onboard sensing and edge analytics to detect and classify boundary crossings. It will fuse detections into a time-stamped, geo-referenced operational picture and shares prioritized alerts, track information, and short video snippets to dispatch and on-scene units to support rapid, coordinated response. When small incursions are identified, SENTRY will be capable of being tasked to maintain positive visual contact, illuminate the indivdual(s), and issue audible warnings to support de-escalation while guiding ground teams. If equipped and authorized, it will be able to employ additional non-lethal effects via integrated deterrent payloads (e.g. optical dazzling or high-power microwave emitters).
